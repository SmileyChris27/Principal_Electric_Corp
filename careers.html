Career Opportunities

Principal Electric Corp is a dynamic and innovative company at the forefront of the electrical engineering and technology sector. The company specializes in the design, development, and implementation of advanced electrical systems, serving both commercial, industrial and residential markets. With a strong commitment to sustainability and cutting-edge technology, Principal Electric Corp is actively involved in projects ranging from smart grid solutions to renewable energy integrations.

Principal Electric Corp is at the forefront of electrical engineering, offering roles in engineering, project management, and more. Join us and impact the future!

By joining us, you become part of a team that values innovation and sustainability. Enjoy competitive salaries, benefits, and a dynamic work environment. Please find a job with your qualifications and apply on our Careers Application Page.
